About TechCon 2024

TechCon is the premier technology conference in Africa, bringing together industry leaders, developers, and tech enthusiasts from around the world. Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has been a hub for innovation, collaboration, and the exchange of cutting-edge ideas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech talent and foster inclusive growth through education, networking, and real-world collaboration. TechCon champions diversity in tech and strives to make innovation accessible to all.

Our History

Over the past decade, TechCon has evolved from a local meetup to an international event attracting over 5,000 attendees annually. With each year, we expand our reach, deepen our impact, and push the boundaries of what’s possible in tech.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ayanna Howard – Renowned roboticist and AI ethics leader
  • Trevor Noah – Host, comedian, and advocate for digital equity in Africa
  • Thoko Mokgosi-Mwantembe – CEO, tech entrepreneur, and trailblazer in African ICT
  • Elon Musk (Virtual Appearance) – CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, discussed innovation in space and AI
  • Rebecca Enonchong – Tech entrepreneur and founder of AppsTech

Why Attend?

TechCon 2024 is more than just talks. Expect hands-on workshops, live demos, start-up pitch battles, and networking opportunities with top minds in the tech industry.
