About TechCon 2024

TechCon is the premier technology conference in Africa, bringing together industry leaders, developers, and tech enthusiasts from around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech talent and foster inclusive growth through education, networking, and real-world collaboration.

Our History

Over the past decade, TechCon has evolved from a local meetup to an international event attracting over 5,000 attendees annually.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ayanna Howard - Renowned roboticist and AI ethics leader
  • Trevor Noah - Host, comedian, and advocate for digital equity in Africa
  • Thoko Mokgosi-Mwantembe - CEO, tech entrepreneur, and ICT trailblazer
  • Elon Musk (Virtual Appearance) - CEO of SpaceX and Tesla
  • Rebecca Enonchong - Tech entrepreneur and founder of AppsTech

Why Attend?

TechCon 2024 is more than just talks. Expect workshops, live demos, start-up pitch battles, and networking opportunities with top minds in the industry.
